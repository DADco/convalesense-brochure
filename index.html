Convalesense

AN INTEGRATED PLATFORM FOR CONDUCTING AND MONITORING PHYSIO IN YOUNG CHILDREN SUFFERING WITH MUSCLE WEAKENING/WASTING DISEASES THROUGH SIMPLE AND FUN GAMES USING SENSOR-ENABLED DEVICES.

Presentation

The Hackathon

What we focused on

Young children with cerebral palsy, muscular dystrophy or spina bifida.

We have focused our efforts helping young children with progressive weakening and/or wasting of muscle diseases, trying to keep them mobile for as long as possible.

Physio is current best practice to:

  • develop coordination
  • build strength
  • improve balance
  • maintain flexibility
  • maximize independence

What we built

  • A web portal for physiotherapists to manage patient exercise plans.
  • Manager App (iOS/Android) An app built to receive the physio exercise plan tailored for the child and to conduct and log sessions of gaming with the child. Data is synchonised back to the physio for tracking progress and tailoring.
  • Sensor App (iOS/Android) An app built for phones and watches that uses motion sensors attached to or held by the child to play the games.
